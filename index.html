Flexible

Your Stack, Your Choice

Enjoy architectural freedom and choose any tool (React, Vue, REST, GraphQL, ...), any rendering strategy (SPA, SSR, SSG) and any deployment strategy (static, server, edge).

Use Vike extensions for automatic tool integration and later, if the need arises, eject for full control over tool integration.

Plug & Play — powered by Vike extensions

Cutting edge, at your own pace

Choose between stable and cutting-edge extensions; go with a conservative stack or live on the edge in unprecedented ways.

Build Your Own Framework

Create an internal company framework that empowers your Software Architects to own the architecture and your Product Developers to quickly iterate.

Flexible by design

From high-level design to the smallest details, Vike is built with flexibility in mind.

Dependable

Adopt the Future

Vike is a flexible and unopinionated core that is open to JavaScript's rapidly evolving ecosystem. Place your bets on a dependable foundation that embraces the future.

Progressively migrate one stack component and one page at a time, instead of big never-ending migrations.

Read: Why Vike > Grow Progressively

Transparent business model

Vike's pricing keeps code 100% open source (MIT licence) and 100% gratis for software engineers while asking companies to pay a small amount, for a transparent and sustainable relationship.

Reliable
